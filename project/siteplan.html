KeyCap Haven - Site Plan

Site Name

KeyCap Haven

This name was chosen to represent a website dedicated to selling custom and premium keycaps for mechanical keyboards. The word "Haven" conveys a place where enthusiasts can find their ideal keycaps.

Site Purpose

KeyCap Haven provides a marketplace for mechanical keyboard enthusiasts to purchase high-quality keycaps. The site will feature:

  • A catalog of available keycaps
  • Customization options
  • Blog posts about mechanical keyboards and keycap trends
  • A contact form for customer support

Scenarios

  • "Where can I find high-quality keycaps for my mechanical keyboard?"
  • "How do I customize my keycaps to fit my keyboard aesthetic?"

Typography

  • Heading Font: Montserrat (used for headings and titles)
  • Body Font: Open Sans (used for paragraphs and descriptions)

Wireframe

The wireframe for both mobile and desktop layouts will include the following elements:

  • Logo and navigation bar at the top
  • Featured keycap collections
  • Product grid showcasing keycaps
  • Blog section for mechanical keyboard trends
  • Contact form and support links at the bottom

Note: Wireframe sketches will be provided separately.
